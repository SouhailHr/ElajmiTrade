About Us

ELAJMI TRADE is located in GAFSA , Tunisia and is part of the Miscellaneous Nondurable Goods Merchant Wholesalers Industry.

Our aim is to provide customers access to quality products and to help manufacturers from Tunisia in their representation to worldwide markets.

In front of the constantly changing international markets, Our Company chose to explore new opportunities to promote the Tunisian products. Thanks to its local and foreign partners, it manages to convey a beautiful image of Tunisia and its wealth through a line of diversified products that concern the North African culture. We assure you of our total commitment to guarantee continuous offers of high quality goods in the shortest time with advantageous prices.

We are committed to our clients' success by delivering competitive pricing via the fairest and most transparent order process in the trading markets. Our main objective is to empower our clients with all the tools required, develop their skills, whilst enjoying an unrivaled trading experience.
